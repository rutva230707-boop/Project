Breathing Exercise

Try this 4–6–8 breathing pattern to calm your body:

1. Breathe in quietly through your nose for 4 seconds.

2. Hold your breath gently for 6 seconds.

3. Exhale slowly through your mouth for 8 seconds.

4. Repeat this cycle 4 times or until you feel more relaxed.
